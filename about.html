Our Story

Founded in 2002 by visionary architects Maria Chen and David Rodriguez, Akshara Designs Architecture & Interiors emerged from a shared passion for creating spaces that harmonize human needs with environmental stewardship. What began as a small studio in Portland has grown into an internationally recognized firm.

Our journey has been marked by a relentless pursuit of innovation, sustainability, and design excellence. From our first LEED-certified project to our current work with carbon-neutral buildings, we've consistently pushed the boundaries of what architecture can achieve.

Today, Akshara Designs stands as a testament to the power of thoughtful design, having completed over 200 projects across residential, commercial, and institutional sectors, each one contributing to a more sustainable and beautiful built environment.
